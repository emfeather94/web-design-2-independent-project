Massage Therapy

Whether you are newly injured, recovering from injury or in need of a tune up, massage therapy sessions are a great way to find problem areas by reducing pain and increasing mobility.

Working 1-1 with the client, Pat will go one step further to develop treatment options to maintain at home to prevent further injury. Pat’s ultimate goal is to have you in as little as possible, because that means he’s gone above and beyond his job to keep your body working at 100%.

First treatment sessions are a 1-hour assessments to discuss past injuries, current problems and tailor treatment to your needs. Follow-up sessions will be discussed, and likely recommended, however this will be dependent on each client.

  • Assessment: 90 min - 120$
  • Follow up: 60 min - 100$
  • Follow up: 45 min - 75$
  • Follow up: 30 min - 65$

    Personal Training

    Looking for training catered to your body’s needs and your athletic goals? Pat has you covered. Whether it’s for building muscle to improve your Olympic lifting, or improving your jump to smash that volleyball, Pat will develop a training plan that works for you.

    It is recommended that a consultation prior to developing a training plan takes place, to ensure a perfect match between coach and athlete. From there, discussion about training will take place, including goals, weaknesses, schedules and payment options.
